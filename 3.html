Jean-Michel Basquiat's Studio

Jean Basquiat lived and worked, he even collaborated with Andy Warhol in this building. A lot of Basquiat's famous Neo-expressionist works were created in this building. However soon things took a turn for the worst when Basquiat's relationship with Warhol worsened. This was because Warhol was his landlord and thus their relationship took a negative turn, they sometimes didnt even want to be seen together. Fun fact this building is now under Angelina Jolies ownership.
